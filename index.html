Volume 02

Modernist Form
Structure is not just a visual construct, but a language.

I. The Intent of Space
Form does not follow function; form and function are entirely unified. We reject the unnecessary and embrace the purposeful. The space left empty is just as vital as the space filled.

II. Color as Weight
A color is never just a hue. It has mass, gravity, and tension. The deep midnight pulls the eye inward, while the terracotta anchors the horizon. Adjusting the lighting shifts the physical reality of the design.
